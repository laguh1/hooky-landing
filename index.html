Features

Everything your craft needs

Built by crafters, for crafters. No bloat, no ads, no login, no subscription for the basics.

Project tracker
Track every piece from cast-on to finished — status, dimensions, photos, and sale info all in one place.
Yarn stash
Know exactly what yarn you have, how much, and where you bought it. No more mystery skeins.
Stitch library
Save your favourite stitches with tutorial links, chart photos and difficulty ratings.
Needle tracker
Know exactly which needles and hook sizes you own. Scan labels with your camera to add them instantly.
Row counter & timer
Tap, speak or type to count rows hands-free. Built-in work timer logs every minute and feeds the price calculator.
Cost & profit
See material costs, labour and profit margin per piece. Know exactly what your work is worth before you price it.
