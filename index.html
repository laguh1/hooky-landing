Features

Everything your craft needs

Built by a crocheter, for crocheters. No bloat, no login, no subscription to use the basics.

🧶
Project tracker
Track every piece from cast-on to finished — status, dimensions, photos, and sale info all in one place.
🎨
Yarn stash
Know exactly what yarn you have, how much, and where you bought it. No more mystery skeins.
📖
Stitch library
Save your favourite stitches with tutorial links, chart uploads, photos and difficulty ratings.
🔢
Row counter
Tap, speak or type. Voice mode counts hands-free so your eyes never leave your work.
⏱️
Work timer
Log every minute automatically. Pause, resume and stop — hours feed straight into the price calculator.
💰
Cost & profit
See material costs, labour and profit margin per piece. Know exactly what your work is worth.
